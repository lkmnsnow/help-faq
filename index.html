Help & FAQ

Q1. What is MyBizzCard?
MyBizzCard is a business card management app that allows you to scan, extract, and organize business card information using offline OCR and optional AI-powered recognition.

Q2. Does MyBizzCard work offline?
Yes. Many features work fully offline, including card scanning, QR scanning, cropped card saving, and manual entry.

Q3. What are AI features?
AI features help extract structured information from scanned cards. When used, data is securely sent for AI processing and returned to your device.

Q4. Is my data stored on servers?
No. Business card images, OCR text, and extracted contact data are not stored on our servers.

Q5. Can OCR or AI make mistakes?
Yes. OCR and AI results may sometimes be inaccurate. You should always review and edit information before saving.

Q6. Are there ads?
The current version of MyBizzCard does not display advertisements.

Q7. How do I manage subscriptions?
Subscriptions are managed through Google Play:
Google Play → Payments & Subscriptions → MyBizzCard

Q8. How can I contact support?
Support email: mybizzcard@gmail.com

Q9. Where can I find legal documents?
Terms of Use and Privacy Policy are available inside the app menu.
