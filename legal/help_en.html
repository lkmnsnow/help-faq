Help & FAQ

HELP & FAQ
Last Updated: 12 January 2026

Q1. What is MyBizzCard?
A1. MyBizzCard is a business card management app that lets you scan, extract, edit, organize, save, and manage business card information using offline OCR and optional AI-powered recognition.

Q2. What does the Free Plan include?
A2. The Free Plan works mostly offline on your device. It includes offline business card scanning, QR code scanning, saving cropped cards, and manual card creation with limited monthly usage. It also includes a small number of lifetime AI-assisted scans.

Q3. What are AI-assisted scans?
A3. AI-assisted scans use artificial intelligence to improve recognition accuracy. Depending on the feature, the app sends either OCR text extracted on your device or a card image to OpenAI for processing. The extracted results are returned to your device.

Q4. What is AI Pro?
A4. AI Pro unlocks advanced AI-powered recognition, higher usage limits, premium features, batch scanning, batch CRM tools, export tools, phonebook save tools, and an ad-free experience.

Q5. What is Batch Scan?
A5. Batch Scan lets you scan multiple business cards into a named batch or list so you can review and manage them together.

Q6. What is Batch CRM?
A6. Batch CRM lets you manage scanned leads using tools such as edit, tags, notes, reminders, filtering, selection, and follow-up support inside the app.

Q7. Can I save scanned contacts to my phonebook?
A7. Yes. You can save individual contacts or selected CRM contacts to your device phonebook. Depending on your device, you may be able to choose the contact account, such as device storage, Google account, or SIM account.

Q8. Can I export my scanned data?
A8. Yes. The app can export selected contact data as CSV, Excel, or VCF, depending on the feature you use.

Q9. Can I send emails from the app?
A9. The app can open your installed email app with selected email addresses for single or batch email actions. The actual sending is handled by your own email app or email provider.

Q10. Is my data stored on MyBizzCard servers?
A10. No. MyBizzCard does not store business card images, OCR text, extracted contact data, CRM notes, tags, reminders, or exported files on its servers after processing. Scanned results are saved only on your device unless you choose to export, share, or save them elsewhere.

Q11. Can AI or OCR make mistakes?
A11. Yes. OCR and AI recognition may sometimes be inaccurate. You should always review and verify all scanned information before saving, exporting, calling, emailing, or sharing it.

Q12. Does the app show advertisements?
A12. The current version of the app does not display third-party advertisements. If ads are introduced in the future, the Privacy Policy will be updated before they are enabled.

Q13. How can I cancel or manage my subscription?
A13. Subscriptions are managed through Google Play. You can cancel or manage your subscription at any time via:
Google Play Store → Payments & Subscriptions → MyBizzCard.

Q14. Is an internet connection required?
A14. Offline features do not require an internet connection. AI-assisted features require an active internet connection.

Q15. Is my permission needed for contacts or phonebook saving?
A15. Yes. If you want to save contacts into your phonebook, the app may request contact permissions on your device.

Q16. Who is responsible for how I use scanned contact data?
A16. You are responsible for making sure you have the right to scan, store, export, email, share, or save another person’s contact information, and for following the laws that apply to you.

Q17. How can I contact support?
A17. You can contact us at: mybizzzcard@gmail.com

Q18. Where can I read the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy?
A18. Both documents are available inside the app under “Terms of Use” and “Privacy Policy”.
